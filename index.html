For Brands

Writing and content strategy for teams who care about voice, systems, and results.

By day, I’m a senior content strategist at a global agency. I help teams untangle messy ecosystems, clarify what they’re saying, and build content that actually gets used.

  • Planning and writing for complex websites and campaigns
  • Content audits and frameworks that make decisions easier
  • Collaborating with UX, design, and performance teams

I’ve worked on brands including Wendy’s, Microsoft, Mazda, Coca-Cola, and others — often in the spaces where storytelling, structure, and data have to sit in the same room.

If you’re interested in collaborating on brand work, you can reach me through the contact form below or via LinkedIn.

About

I am a writer based in Kansas City, Missouri. I received my MFA in creative writing from the University of Tennessee, and my work has appeared in Bayou Magazine, Weave Magazine, Defenestration, Bear Review, and other publications.

Outside of fiction, I work as a senior content strategist, helping teams clarify what they’re saying and how it reaches people. That background tends to shape how I think about stories too — structure, attention, and the little details people carry around.

I also occasionally make ASMR videos under the name pAigeSMR. You can find them here if you’re into that kind of thing.
