About

Fiction first. Many hats, same brain.

I’m a writer and ASMR creator based in Kansas City. I hold an MFA in creative writing from the University of Tennessee, and my fiction and poems have appeared in Bayou Magazine, Weave Magazine, Defenestration, Bear Review, and elsewhere. My microfiction “This Morning I Was French” is forthcoming in Scaffold.

By day, I work as a senior content strategist, helping teams clarify what they’re saying and why it matters. That same curiosity and systems-brain shows up in my fiction: I’m interested in how people narrate themselves, what they choose to believe, and the tiny cracks where reality starts to feel a little off.

Other Projects

The rest of the orbit: ASMR, live performance, and content strategy.

I make ASMR videos and livestreams as pAigeSMR, where I play with character, intimacy, sound, and quiet weirdness. It’s another way of exploring voice and closeness—just with a microphone instead of the page.

I also perform improv and sketch comedy, which keeps my writing loose, collaborative, and very alive. A lot of my favorite sentences started as something I said onstage and couldn’t quite let go of.
